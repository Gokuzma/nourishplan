Present the following verification checklist to the user for manual testing of all INVT requirements.

**Pre-requisite:** Log in as demo@nourishplan.test at https://nourishplan.gregok.ca

**1. Navigation (INVT-01)**
- Open mobile drawer (hamburger menu) -- verify "Inventory" appears between Meals and Household
- Tap "Inventory" -- verify /inventory page loads with Pantry/Fridge/Freezer tabs
- On desktop: verify Sidebar shows "Inventory" nav item

**2. Add item (INVT-01, INVT-02, INVT-03)**
- Tap "Add Item" button
- Fill in: Name "Chicken Breast", Quantity 500, Unit "g", Location "Fridge", Expiry tomorrow's date, Price 8.99
- Check "Mark as staple" checkbox
- Tap "Add to Inventory"
- Verify item appears in Fridge tab with expiry badge
- Add another item with no expiry -- verify no badge shown
- Add an item with a past expiry date -- verify red "Expired" badge

**3. Edit and remove (INVT-01)**
- Tap an item row -- verify it expands showing Edit and Remove buttons
- Tap Edit -- verify modal opens pre-filled with item data
- Change quantity, tap "Save Changes" -- verify updated
- Tap another item, tap Remove -- verify confirmation with "Used" and "Discarded" options
- Choose "Used" -- verify item disappears from list

**4. Barcode scanning (INVT-04)**
- Tap "Scan" button in Inventory header
- If camera access prompt appears, allow it
- Scan a physical product barcode (e.g., any food package)
- Verify name and brand auto-fill in the add form
- If no physical barcode available: enter barcode "3017620422003" (Nutella) manually, tap "Look up"
- Verify name populates from Open Food Facts

**5. Quick Scan (INVT-04, D-16)**
- Tap "Quick Scan" button
- Scan multiple items in sequence (or use manual entry for each)
- Verify running list of scanned items appears
- Tap "Done" -- verify all items appear in inventory

**6. Cook deduction (INVT-05)**
- Navigate to a recipe that has ingredients matching items in your inventory
- Tap "Mark as Cooked"
- Verify the deduction receipt panel appears at the bottom showing:
  - Which items were deducted with amounts and purchase dates
  - Which ingredients were missing from inventory
- Navigate to Inventory -- verify quantities were reduced

**7. Home page widget (D-22)**
- Navigate to Home page
- Verify "Inventory" card section shows location counts
- If items have upcoming expiry dates, verify "Expiring soon" list appears
- Tap the link to Inventory -- verify navigation works

**8. Expiry sort order (INVT-03)**
- On Inventory page, verify items are sorted by expiry date (soonest first)
- Verify expired items appear at top with red badge
- Verify items with no expiry appear at the bottom

**9. Dark mode**
- Toggle dark mode in Settings
- Verify Inventory page, expiry badges, modals, and widget all render correctly in dark mode
